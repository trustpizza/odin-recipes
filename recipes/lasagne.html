Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is a pasta dish which has been enjoyed for millenia.

It consists of flat sheets of pasta alternated by fillings, such as meats or cheeses

Ingredients

  • 1 package Anna Traditional Lasagne (1lb)
  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 jar Cento All Purpose Pasta Sauce (26oz)
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 Egg, beaten
  • 1 lb Ricotta Cheese
  • 8 oz Mozzarella Cheese, grated
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan Cheese, grated
  • 1 tbsp Cento Parsley
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Farenheit
  2. Prepare lasagne according to directions on package; drain and set aside
  3. In a saucepan, brown ground beef; drain fat
  4. In a large bowl, blend egg, ricotta, mozarella, parmesan and parsley
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste
  6. Spread a layer of 1/4 sauce mixture in the bottom of a 13 inch x 9 inch x 2 inch pan
  7. Arrange noodles lengthwise side by side, overlapping slightly to cover the sauce
  8. Layer 1/2 cheese mixture, then 1/4 sauce mixture, top with noodles and repeat
  9. Cover with remaining sauce mixture and remaining 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  10. Cover and bake for 45 minutes
  11. Let stand for 5-10 minutes before serving
